HOST MURDER MYSTERY: BAND MYSTERY

A mystery for 7 to 9 players.

Description

To celebrate their latest hit single, the world famous band THE SCREAMING DIAMONDS host an exclusive launch party on a private boat for the winners of their 'Biggest Fan' competition. The night gets a bit crazy, and everybody wakes up the next morning with hazy memories and hangovers. But then someone discovers the lead singer and guitarist of the band is dead!

How to host this murder mystery

    Before the Game

  1. Read through the below descriptions of each of the characters. Pick which of your friends will be each character (either randomly or by who suits)
  2. Send them their matching character code and this link HERE where they can read up on their characters and the game before you play

    3. During the Game

  3. All players (including you) keep their player guide open HERE
  4. Start by getting everyone to introduce themselves and their role
  5. There are five rounds of clues to get revealed. When everyone is ready for the next clue, you as the host can find the next code at the bottom of this page. Get everybody to type it in the code together and read their clues - each round their is a PUBLIC clue for all players to see, and a PRIVATE clue which is different for each player (that they can reveal or keep secret as they wish)
  6. Keep playing till all the clues have been revealed. At the end once everyone is satisfied talking, get all players to right down their recommendation, and reveal them at the same time. Then click HERE to see the final ending...
