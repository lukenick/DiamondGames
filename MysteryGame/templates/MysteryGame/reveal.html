A Confession

After hours of arguing and accusations among the ship's passengers, finally George cracks and starts to confess:

"I didn't mean to do it, it was self-defense."

"At around 2:30am I tried to talk to Rick about how I was planning to leave the band and go solo, but he just started getting angry and accused me of sleeping with his girlfriend. It got a bit heated, so I left after we started yelling."

"Two hours later I bumped into him again in the pool room, and he started getting angry again, accusing me of 'trying to steal credit for his genius and steal his girlfiend'. I started yelling back, so he pushed me up against the bookcase. I grabbed an award statue from the shelf and hit him across the head in self-defence."

"I hit him a few more times before I realised what I had done. I quickly threw the trophy off the edge of the boat, and snuck to bed with a bottle of wine to cover up my guilt."

"I didn't mean to kill it, but I did enjoy it"
