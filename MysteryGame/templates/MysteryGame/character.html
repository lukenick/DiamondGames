Murder on the High C

Welcome {{this_character.name}}

The Story So Far

Last night the SS Pennyworth set sail with nine people onboard - the four members of the wildly successful band 'Screaming Diamonds', along with their manager, publicist, one of their girlfriends, the two winners of a 'biggest fan' competition and of course the ships captain. After a wild night of celebrating the release of the latest album, everybody eventually went to bed in the early hours of the morning. You have all emerged the next day, tired and hungover to discover that the Lead Singer and Guitarist of the band Rick Diamond is dead!
His body lies in the boat's poolroom/study, surrounded by shards of broken glass and a broken window. There is blood pooled around his head, but other than that there isn't any obvious evidence. Who killed Rick? Everybody is a suspect so watch your back!

How To Play

You are one of nine characters, all trying to work out who is behind the murder and what else went on last night. Below you have information on each character - both information that everybody has, and information that just you have. As the game progresses your host will reveal clues that provide new information for everybody, and you will also receive new clues for just your character. You can choose to reveal any of this information or to keep it secret, whatever you think will better suit your character and their goals.
